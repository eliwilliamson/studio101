Jason DolanColor Director & Owner

Friendly, tattooed, and incredibly good at what he does, Jason boasts a large and devoted following, including many actors. He brings life and luster to any shade of hair, yet he has a particular talent of giving highlights to black Asian hair that come out caramel, not red. Anyone who knows this conundrum will—and should—be wowed by Jason.

Stephanie SheySenior Stylist

One of Studio 101's most vivacious and dynamic personalities, Stephanie has been trained over the course of her decade long career by the likes of L'Oreal and Redken and brings national recognition to Studio 101, having had her talents featured by the likes of leading beauty publication, Allure Magazine. Stephanie's focus spans both color and cut and her passion for making her clients look and feel as beautiful as possible is something that drives her on a daily basis.

Steven LesterSenior Stylist

Steven's quest for inspiration has taken him all over the world, arming him with an elite sense of style cherished by his loyal client base across New England and beyond. Cutting his teeth as a barber and male stylist, Steven merged into the color and style space nearly 15 years ago, including training stints with L'Oreal and Redken. Steven has earned a well deserved reputation as a cutting edge technician and beauty expert bringing smiles to any client who graces his chair.

Kristen Tetreault-LazzareschiSenior Stylist
